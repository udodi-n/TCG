### ✦ Prompt Templates

**Basic:**

> "Using the headline swipe file above, write 10 headlines for [your product/offer]. My audience is [describe them]. The main benefit is [what they get]."

**Specific formula:**

> "Using the swipe file above, write me a headline in the style of 'They Laughed When I Sat Down at the Piano' for [my product]. My audience is [describe them]."

**Multiple variations:**

> "Using the swipe file above, give me 5 headline variations for [product/offer] — one that leads with curiosity, one with fear, one with a specific result, one with a story hook, and one that calls out my audience directly."

**Email subject lines:**

> "Using the headline formulas in the swipe file above, write 10 email subject lines for [topic/offer]. Keep them under 8 words each."

**Deep brief (best results):**

> "Using the swipe file above, write headlines for my offer. Here's my brief:
>
> - Product: [what it is]
> - Audience: [who they are, their pain]
> - Big promise: [the result they get]
> - Timeframe: [how fast]
> - Unique mechanism: [what makes it different]
> - Tone: [aggressive/warm/curiosity-driven/story-led]"
