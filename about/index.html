Origin story

I chose words over wrenches.

Fresh out of high school, my mum had a plan for me: mechanic apprenticeship. Solid career. Steady hands. Grease under your nails. Not exactly what I had in mind.

I didn't despise the idea. But I knew it wouldn't take me anywhere close to where I wanted to go. So I took a gap year, found copywriting, and got completely obsessed with it

Then I tried to find a Discord copywriting community few months into my journey. All I found were ocean of bots and ghost towns. So I did what any unemployed bored teen on a gap year would do—I built my own.

360+ copywriters later, The Copy Grind exists. The newsletter came after. And here we are.

What I do

Fitness Coaches
